Why the Ambassador?

The Hindustan Ambassador isn't just a car; it's an Indian icon that ruled the streets for half a century. We decided to pay homage to this grand legend by transforming its signature spacious boot into Udupi's most advanced custom-engineered mobile charcoal barbecue rig.

By combining nostalgic vintage vibes with state-of-the-art grilling techniques, we create a dining experience that is simple, premium, and absolutely unforgettable.
